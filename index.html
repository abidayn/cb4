SMAN Nusantara Raya
CB4 Reference Case · Fictional school, realistic data
CB4 Studi Kasus
SMAN Nusantara Raya is a public high school in a mid-sized Indonesian city with 420 Grade 12 students in 2025. Of those, 87 students were declared eligible for SNBP (the national school achievement pathway to public universities). However, the school's guidance counsellor (Guru BK) has never maintained structured alumni data — no record of who got into which university, with what grades. All students chose their university and study programme based on intuition, parental advice, or social media trends. The school has never systematically tracked this history. As a result, only 19 out of 87 eligible students passed SNBP (21.8%) — just below the national average of ~22% for academic state universities. Worse, the Grade 11 cohort becoming eligible next year has no idea where to begin.
